# Day 4: Network Scanning with Nmap 🕵️‍♀️

## Introduction
On Day 4 of my cybersecurity journey, I learned how to use Nmap, a powerful network scanning tool used to discover open ports and services on a system.

---

## What is Nmap?
Nmap (Network Mapper) is a tool used by cybersecurity professionals to scan networks, identify open ports, and detect running services.

---

## Scans I Performed

### Basic Scan
nmap 127.0.0.1


### Stealth Scan
nmap -sS 127.0.0.1

---

## What I Observed
- The host (my system) was active and reachable
- All scanned ports were closed
- No open ports were detected

This showed that my system was not running any exposed services at the time of the scan.

## What I Learned

- What ports are and why they matter
- How attackers might discover vulnerabilities
