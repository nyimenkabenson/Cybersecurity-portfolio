# Day 6: Basic Security Audit 🛡️

## Introduction
On Day 6 of my cybersecurity journey, I performed a basic security audit on my system. The goal was to identify open ports and running services using Nmap.

---

## What is a Security Audit?
A security audit involves evaluating a system to identify potential vulnerabilities, open ports, and exposed services.

---

## Tools Used
- Nmap

---

## Scan Performed
- nmap -sS -sV 127.0.0.1


---

## Findings
- The host was active and reachable
- No open ports were detected
- No services were exposed

---

## Analysis
The system appears to be secure by default, as there are no open ports or running services that could be exploited.

---

## What I Learned
- How to perform a basic security audit
- How to interpret scan results
- The importance of minimizing exposed services
